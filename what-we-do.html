What we do

Redshift Strategy provides strategic advice, investment services and insight into rapid technological change in the media and telecommunications industries

Strategy

Redshift provides strategic advice at the leading edge of the technical and commercial developments in the connected digital world. With deep sector knowledge and experience, we work with leading stakeholders across the telecoms, internet, media and digitised sectors to help them achieve on-going growth through effective strategies in network infrastructure, content, data analytics and monetisation, customer engagement, investments and partnerships.

The Redshift team have been strategy advisors and senior executives in these sectors for over 20 years and have worked on hundreds of projects all over the world.

Examples of our projects

For a UK data platform, conducting their first ever big data review for optimising their operations, technology choices, customer engagement strategy and revenues
Developing the 5 year strategy for a major UK production super-indie covering their product portfolio, growth and business operations
For a major satellite business, developing forecasts for the demand for hi-definition TV and space segment requirements for 2020
For a leading international production company, developing the strategy for their Middle East operation
For an internet provider, creating the business and financial plan for their network roll-out in rural areas
For a VoD platform, conducting a product portfolio and strategic review leading to recommendations for future business activities
For a global telecoms organisation, scenario planning and financial forecasting to identify future pools of value and strategies required for sustainable value growth
For a US technology company, international growth strategy and business development of an enterprise IoT application for facilities management
For a mobile operator, valuation of 800MHz and 2600MHz bandwidth based on multiple demand forecasts
For a telecoms and CDN provider, conducting a product and profitability review and forecast for content delivery management services

Investment

Redshift provides investment advisory and due diligence services across the telecoms, internet, media and digitised sectors. We pride ourselves in our deep sector expertise and wide contact network to ensure that we best serve our clients to capitalise on new opportunities on both sides of investments.

Examples of our projects

Creating a new business/financial plan on broadband growth for the Chairman and CEO or a major regional group
For an investor, Redshift is preparing a business plan for the launch of a new entertainment platform and concept that will exploit both traditional broadcasting platforms together with connected TV options
For an investor, Redshift has created a financial and business plan for a diversified investment strategy across film production, drama and musical content
Due diligence of a major UK infrastructure provider, valuing all of the products and services across the terrestrial broadcast, mobile, satellite and other businesses
Redshift is providing strategic and investment advisory support to a UK ISP through second round funding and subsequent acquisition of a competitor
For a group of young entrepreneurs, Redshift is supporting the development of a business plan and IM to secure seed investment from the venture capital community
To support a due diligence, conducted a global benchmarking study of a number of leading pay TV operators around the world

Insight

In an increasingly software driven world, customer engagement is becoming the key asset to drive business growth and value. Understanding how customers engage with your service across multiple platforms is increasingly a pre-requisite for driving value.

Redshift provides advice to companies on how to use their data to better understand their customers and drive future value, as well as opportunities to develop strategies built of personal data which deliver growth whilst protecting customer privacy.
